Loosh AI

Emotionally intelligent agents for the real world.

AI today is emotionally blind.

Language models are powerful, but they don't understand. They can't detect sarcasm, read between the lines, or sense distress.

This is a critical failure. Deploying emotionally unaware AI in high-trust roles like healthcare, robotics, or personal companionship is not just ineffective—it's dangerous. Trust is broken, and real-world harm can occur when context is missed.

We are building AI that understands humanity.

Loosh AI is a new class of artificial intelligence: emotionally and ethically intelligent agents.

We've built a decentralized cognitive architecture, modeled on the human mind, that integrates layered reasoning, emotional inference, and narrative memory. Our agents don't just predict the next word; they understand context, feel empathy, and learn from experience.

We replicated the architecture of the mind, not just the output.

Our system isn't a single, monolithic model. It's a decentralized network of specialized cognitive modules running on Bittensor.

  • Emotional Inference Layer (MIME): Our core innovation. It analyzes multimodal signals (voice, text, expression) to decode unspoken emotional context.
  • Cognitive Subsystems: A distributed mesh of models for specific tasks like ethical reasoning, anticipation, and self-inhibition.
  • Active Cognition & Memory: An executive layer that orchestrates the subsystems, makes decisions, and learns from a persistent, narrative memory.

Building the future, today.

  • Product: Core subnet architecture and validator code is complete. Working prototype demonstrates real-time emotional inference.
  • Timeline: On track for TestNet deployment in Q3 2025.
  • Partnerships: In active discussions with three leading consciousness research institutes (Monroe Institute, IONS) to secure proprietary neurological data for model training.

The market isn't just chatbots; it's every human-machine interaction.

The AI Agent market is projected to be a $13 Trillion industry by 2030. We are targeting two beachhead markets where emotional intelligence is the most critical missing piece:

  • Human-Centric Robotics ($150B Market): For assistive, companion, and service robots that require safe and natural interaction.
  • Telehealth & Digital Wellness ($75B Market): For therapy, coaching, and patient monitoring agents that require genuine empathy and trust.

Cognition-as-a-Service (CaaS).

We provide access to our agents through a scalable, usage-based API. Our revenue is transparent and value-aligned.

  • API Usage: Tiered pricing based on minutes of cognition consumed (Basic Inference, Intermediate Cognition, Advanced Empathy).
  • Device Licensing: An annual license for embodied systems (robotics) that require edge deployment.

No ads. No selling data. Just best-in-class cognitive services.
