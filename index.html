Engineering from First Principles

Our robust architectural vision is grounded in cognitive science, affective computing, and embodied intelligence. We have defined a modular framework for building emotionally aware agents.

  • Emotional Inference: A core module for analyzing multimodal signals (EEG, nonverbal cues) to decode unspoken emotional context.
  • Cognitive Subsystems: A distributed mesh of task-specific models for compositional inference, ethical reasoning, and anticipation.
  • Active Cognition & Inhibition: An executive function layer that orchestrates tasks, provides context-sensitive self-inhibition, and enables self-reflection.
  • Narrative Memory: A persistent data model for identity, enabling agents to learn and evolve from lived experience.
